About Three of Cups

Building authentic connections through intentional matchmaking

Our Mission

Three of Cups is a unique friendship matchmaking platform where meaningful connections are formed through intentional pairings by a licensed therapist. We believe that friendship is the foundation of community, and we're dedicated to helping people find their perfect friendship matches.

Our comprehensive assessment process goes beyond surface-level compatibility, diving deep into personality traits, values, communication styles, and friendship readiness to create matches that have the potential to flourish into lasting friendships.
